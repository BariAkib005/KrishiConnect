About KrishiConnect

Empowering Bangladesh's Agricultural Future

We bridge the gap between farmers and markets, providing financial inclusion and technology-driven solutions for sustainable agriculture.

Our Mission

A Fair & Connected Agricultural Ecosystem

KrishiConnect was founded with a simple belief: every Bangladeshi farmer deserves fair access to markets and financial services. We eliminate middlemen, reduce exploitation, and bring transparency to agricultural trade.

Direct Trade

Connecting farmers directly to institutional and individual buyers

Financial Inclusion

Microfinance loans designed specifically for agricultural needs

Sustainable Growth

Promoting organic farming and environmentally conscious practices
