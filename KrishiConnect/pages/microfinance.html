Build Your Credit Score

Improve your access to larger loans by maintaining a good repayment history

How Credit Score Works

Your KrishiConnect credit score ranges from 1 to 5 stars and is calculated based on:

  • Repayment History: Timely repayments improve your score
  • Sales Performance: Consistent sales on the marketplace
  • Profile Completeness: Detailed farm and personal information
  • Buyer Ratings: Positive feedback from buyers

A higher credit score gives you access to larger loan amounts, better interest rates, and faster approval processes.
